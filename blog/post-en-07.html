Strong Bones After 40

Veröffentlicht: 10.02.2026

After 40, bone health becomes especially important. Here's how to stay strong!

What Happens to Bones?

Bone density naturally decreases. With the right lifestyle, you can positively influence this.

The 3 Pillars of Strong Bones

  • Calcium - dairy, leafy greens, fish

  • Vitamin D - sunlight, fatty fish, eggs

  • Movement - strength training, walking, dancing

    • Simple Daily Habits

  • 30 minutes of walking daily

  • Take stairs instead of elevator

  • Dancing as a hobby

    • The Bottom Line

    Strong bones mean more quality of life into old age!
