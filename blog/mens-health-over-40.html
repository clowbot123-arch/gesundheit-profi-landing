Men's Health Over 40: Key Priorities

Updated: Feb 14, 2026 • 8 min read

Health after 40 improves quickly with a few high-impact habits.

Core Health Metrics

Track blood pressure, waist circumference, and sleep.

  • Annual bloodwork
  • Strength training weekly
  • Limit alcohol frequency

Sustainable Routine

Use a realistic baseline routine you can keep year-round.

  • Meal prep once weekly
  • Fixed training slots
  • Night routine for sleep
