Building Muscle After 40 - Yes, It's Possible!

Veröffentlicht: 10.02.2026

Many think building muscle after 40 is impossible. That's a myth!

Why it works

With the right training and nutrition, you can achieve great results at 40+.

3 Simple Tips

  • Progressive overload - gradually increase weights

  • More protein - about 1.6g per kilogram of body weight

  • Quality sleep - 7-8 hours for optimal recovery

    • The Bottom Line

    Consistency is key. Small daily improvements add up to big results!
