Sleep - The Underrated Muscle Booster

Veröffentlicht: 10.02.2026

Sleep is THE most important factor for muscle building - don't underestimate it!

Why Sleep Matters

  • Growth hormones are released during sleep

  • Muscle recovery happens primarily during sleep

  • Cortisol (stress hormone) decreases with good sleep

    • Optimize Your Sleep

  • Plan 7-8 hours

  • Cool, dark bedroom

  • No screens 1 hour before bed

  • Consistent sleep schedule

    • The Bottom Line

    Without good sleep, even the best training won't help much. Sleep is free - use it!
