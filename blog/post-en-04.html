Staying Vital After 40

Veröffentlicht: 10.02.2026

Your body changes with age - but that doesn't mean stopping being active!

What Changes?

  • Metabolism slows down

  • Muscle mass decreases (if not trained)

  • Recovery takes longer

    • What You Can Do

    ✅ Regular exercise - strength training 2-3x per week
    ✅ Balanced nutrition - more protein, less sugar
    ✅ Stress management - yoga, meditation, hobbies
    ✅ Preventive checkups - regular doctor visits

    The Bottom Line

    Vitality is not a matter of age, but lifestyle!
